Here's a list of interesting open source (or free) projects and resources 
that are related to Multi-Value databases like ScarletDME, OpenQM, D3 Pick, 
UniVerse, etc.

[26Mar21] Started.
AnyKey - https://github.com/MarcusAureliusRhodes/AnyKey
AnyKey's purpose is to enable Pick programs to respond to any key (or 
combination of keys) on the keyboard, no matter what terminal emulation or 
terminal emulator is being used.

Introduction to Pick Programming: https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCN7Pq68IP_ffi9jokzzpnHg
